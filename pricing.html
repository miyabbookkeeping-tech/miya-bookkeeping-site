What's included

What monthly bookkeeping always covers.

  • Accurate categorization

    Every transaction is reviewed and categorized against a chart of accounts tailored to your business or personal setup.

  • Monthly reconciliation

    Every bank, credit card, and applicable merchant account is reconciled to statements each month.

  • Organized reporting

    Financial statements delivered monthly, with comparative views as needed and commentary on anything worth noting.

  • Proactive communication

    We ask the clarifying questions, flag anomalies early, and keep you informed without flooding your inbox.
